# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: September 25, 2025_

Cerbi LLC (“Cerbi”, “we”, “us”) provides developer tooling for structured logging, governance, and observability.

## What we collect
- **Site analytics**: page views, referrers, anonymized events (GA4).
- **Contact form**: name, email, company, message, and any attachments you send.
- **Product telemetry (optional)**: if enabled in Cerbi libraries, counts and error codes only; no PII by default.

## How we use data
- To respond to inquiries, provide services, improve the site and product, and meet legal requirements.

## Sharing
We do not sell personal data. We may share with service providers under contract (e.g., hosting, analytics) or as required by law.

## Data retention
We keep data as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or anonymize it.
