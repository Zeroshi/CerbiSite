What ScoreImpact Represents

ScoreImpact is the governance score Cerbi generates from policy outcomes; Cerbi never brokers or forwards your log traffic.

Coverage

Shows how much of your fleet is under governed schemas, so gaps surface before incidents.

Violations

Counts policy breaks (like unredacted secrets or dropped samples) and weights them by severity.

Redaction Posture

Measures how consistently sensitive fields are masked at source and downstream.

Risk Trend Signals

Highlights whether risk is climbing or falling, weighting recent drift and noisy services.

Why Scoring Reduces Operational Uncertainty

  • Makes schema drift visible with coverage and violation deltas instead of ad-hoc checks.
  • Surfaces audit evidence gaps by showing where masking and enforcement are missing.
  • Focuses remediation on the noisiest routes, so reliability work is predictable.
  • All scoring happens without routing or storing your payloads.

Safe Rollout With Relax Mode

  • Relax mode enforces masking and schema checks without dropping traffic, so teams can observe impact before tightening.
  • Visibility shows which services improve the score and which need fixes before blocking.
  • Scores stay consistent across routers—Cerbi never brokers, stores, or reroutes your logs.

FAQ

Is this ML?

No. Scores are deterministic from policy outcomes.

Does Cerbi store my raw logs?

No. Cerbi scores governance signals only.

Does Cerbi route logs?

No. Your routers keep forwarding data; Cerbi does not broker or fan-out traffic.

Do I need a specific router?

No. Any router works as long as it emits governance signals.
