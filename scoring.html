What ScoreImpact Represents

ScoreImpact summarizes how healthy your logging posture is across every service.

Coverage

Tracks how many workloads are under governance, so gaps show up before incidents.

Violations

Counts policy breaks like unredacted secrets or dropped samples, weighted by severity.

Redaction Posture

Measures how consistently sensitive fields are masked at the edge and downstream.

Risk Trends

Shows whether risk is climbing or falling over time, with weight on recent drift.

Why Scoring Helps

  • Highlights weak spots early, so fixes happen before misconfigurations spill into production.
  • Prioritizes what to fix first by tying scores to the highest-risk services and traffic paths.
  • Provides a common signal during reviews, reducing noisy alerts and surprise outages.

Cross-Service Standardization

  • Uses the same scoring rubric for every team, so policies are enforced evenly without manual checklists.
  • Signals when services diverge from shared baselines, making drift visible without ticket policing.
  • Lets platform teams set defaults once while allowing services to self-correct against the score.

FAQ

Is this ML?

No. Scores are rule-based and deterministic so operators can audit every factor.

Does Cerbi store my logs?

No. Cerbi evaluates metadata and policy outcomes; log storage stays in your stack.

Does Cerbi route logs?

No. Cerbi observes routing decisions but does not broker or forward log traffic.

Do I need a specific router?

No. Scoring works with any router that can emit governance signals and metrics.
