Why CerbiStream Exists

As teams scale, logging splinters across frameworks, teams, contractors, and third-party libs. You end up with inconsistent payloads, missed fields, and surprise PII in production. Audits, compliance, and insights suffer.

1. Enforce Structure at the Source

2. Modular by Design

3. Audit-Ready Compliance
