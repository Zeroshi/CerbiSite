Why CerbiStream Exists

As applications scale across teams and vendors, logs drift: names differ, fields go missing, and sensitive data leaks. CerbiSuite keeps logs structured, governed, and portable‚Äîso your downstream tools (Splunk, ELK, Datadog, OpenSearch, etc.) receive the **right** data with **consistent** shape.

1) Enforce Structure at the Source

Define log schemas once. CerbiStream validates fields and types at build-time and runtime before emission for consistent output.

2) Logger-Agnostic

Use CerbiStream, or keep Serilog/NLog/MEL and add governance through plugins. Same profiles, same rules.

3) Audit-Ready Compliance

Required/forbidden fields, redaction, and encryption help align with HIPAA, GDPR, SOC-2 and internal policy.
