Why CerbiStream Exists

As apps grow and teams span squads, contractors, and third-party libraries, logs become inconsistent. That hurts audits, privacy, SRE, and data science. Cerbi fixes logging at the source‚Äîbefore data fans out to Splunk/ELK/Datadog/etc.

1) Enforce Structure

Define required/forbidden fields, enumerations, types, redactions, and tagging. Validate at build-time and runtime.

2) Same Profiles Everywhere

Profiles live in CerbiShield and drive analyzers, plugins, and the UI. Change once, roll everywhere.

3) Audit & ML-Ready

Logs arrive clean and predictable, reducing breach risk and powering downstream analytics.

Cerbi Vision & Phases

Phase 1 ‚Äî CerbiSuite (Now)

  • CerbiStream: structured logger with encryption, file-fallback, and governance hooks.
  • CerbiStream.GovernanceAnalyzer: Roslyn analyzer for build-time enforcement in .NET.
  • Cerbi.Serilog.GovernanceAnalyzer: runtime validation for teams on Serilog today.
  • CerbiShield: governance UI, RBAC, profile store, validation APIs, and deployment.

Goal: ship consistent, compliant logs by default‚Äîbefore they reach your log platform.

Phase 2 ‚Äî Downstream Intelligence

  • CerbIQ (routing/normalization): fan-out, enrichment, and partition-aware rollups.
  • CerbiSense (ML): anomaly detection, drift alerts, and policy effectiveness scoring.

We are not trying to replace Splunk/ELK/Datadog/etc. Cerbi improves what flows into them and adds governance you can prove.

The Cerbi Ecosystem

A cohesive suite where each component addresses a critical aspect of enterprise logging.

ComponentStatusPurpose
CerbiStream GA Core .NET logger with structured JSON output, encryption (AES/Base64), file fallback, and first-class governance hooks. Emits ML-friendly metadata and supports profile reloads.
CerbiStream.GovernanceAnalyzer GA Roslyn analyzer that surfaces diagnostics at build-time: required/forbidden fields, enums, types, redaction, and tagging. Prevent violations before they ship.
Cerbi.Serilog.GovernanceAnalyzer GA Runtime governance for Serilog. Validate and tag violations on the fly without dropping logs. Ideal for teams standardizing gradually without changing their logger overnight.
CerbiShield Beta (SaaS soon) Governance dashboard with RBAC, profile store, validation APIs, deployment history, and audit trails. Push profiles to file systems, GitHub, cloud storage, or runtime consumers.

CerbiSense Phase 2 Planned ML-driven anomaly detection and trend forecasting on clean, governed data. SLO/SLI friendly signals with privacy-aware features.

Governance That Meets Teams Where They Are

Use Serilog today with the plugin, or modernize with CerbiStream. Either way, enforcement runs from the same governance profiles in CerbiShield.

Build-Time Enforcement

Roslyn analyzer catches violations in IDE/CI. Developers see actionable diagnostics with rule IDs and suggested fixes.

Runtime Enforcement

Serilog plugin or CerbiStream runtime engine validates logs without dropping events, tagging violations for search.

Compliance Starter Kits

Jump-start HIPAA/GDPR/SOC-2-aligned profiles and refine per policy. Keep evidence with deployment history.

  "version": "1.3.0",
  "required": ["timestamp", "level", "message", "app", "tenantId"],
